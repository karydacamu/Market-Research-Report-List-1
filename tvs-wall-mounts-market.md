<p><p>The TV wall mount market offers various types to cater to different needs. Fixed mounts securely hold TVs flat against the wall for a clean look but limit positioning. Tilt mounts allow slight upward or downward adjustments for better viewing angles, especially in high placements. Full motion mounts provide versatile positioning, allowing the TV to swivel and extend from the wall for optimal viewing. Fixed easy-mount options feature simple installation processes while maintaining a sleek design without extensive adjustment capabilities.</p></p>
