<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems are essential for quality control in manufacturing processes, particularly in electronics. The market is divided into two main types: 2D AOI systems and 3D AOI systems. 2D AOI systems use a single camera to capture images and identify defects based on predefined criteria, making them cost-effective for surface inspections. In contrast, 3D AOI systems utilize multiple cameras and advanced algorithms to provide a more comprehensive evaluation of component height and volume, effectively detecting subtle defects and ensuring higher accuracy in inspections.</p></p>
