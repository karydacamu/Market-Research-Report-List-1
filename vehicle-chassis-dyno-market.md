<p><strong>The Vehicle Chassis Dyno Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi Roller</li><li>Single Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle chassis dynamometer market comprises two primary types: multi-roller and single roller systems. Multi-roller dynos utilize multiple rollers to simulate a vehicle's road conditions, allowing for accurate testing of power output and exhaust emissions under various loads. In contrast, single roller dynos employ a single roller setup, which is generally more compact and less costly, making it suitable for lighter vehicles or specific testing applications. Both types are essential for performance evaluation and regulatory compliance in the automotive industry.</p></p>
