<p><p>The autonomous vehicle sensors market comprises key types: camera sensors, radar sensors, and Lidar sensors. Camera sensors capture visual information, enabling object recognition and lane detection; they are essential for interpreting the driving environment. Radar sensors use radio waves to detect objects, offering robust performance in various weather conditions, particularly for measuring distances and speeds. Lidar sensors employ laser pulses to create intricate 3D maps of the surroundings, providing high precision in object detection and spatial awareness, which are crucial for safe navigation.</p></p>
