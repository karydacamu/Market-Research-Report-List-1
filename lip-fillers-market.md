<p><p>The lip fillers market primarily includes two types: Hyaluronic Acid (HA) lip fillers and Poly Lactic Acid (PLA) lip fillers. HA fillers are popular for their ability to provide instant volume and hydration, offering natural-looking results that typically last six months to a year. In contrast, PLA fillers stimulate collagen production over time, leading to gradual volume enhancement and longevity of up to two years. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs in aesthetics and age-related volume loss.</p></p>
