<p><p>Explosion-proof resistance thermometers are essential for accurate temperature measurement in hazardous environments. The market is segmented into three types based on wiring configurations: 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire. </p><p>2-wire thermometers are simpler and cost-effective but can introduce measurement errors due to lead resistance. </p><p>3-wire designs offer improved accuracy by compensating for lead resistance, making them more reliable. </p><p>4-wire configurations provide the highest accuracy by completely eliminating lead resistance effects, ideal for precision applications in explosive environments.</p></p>
