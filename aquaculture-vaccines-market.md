<p><p>The aquaculture vaccines market consists of three main types: oral vaccines, injectable vaccines, and immersion vaccines. Oral vaccines are administered through feed, allowing fish to ingest the vaccine conveniently. Injectable vaccines require direct administration via injection, ensuring targeted protection for individual animals. Immersion vaccines involve soaking fish in a vaccine solution, enabling mass vaccination of large groups. Each delivery method has unique advantages and applications, contributing to the overall health management of aquatic species in aquaculture settings.</p></p>
