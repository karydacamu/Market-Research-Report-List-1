<p><p>RFID card cabinet locks are categorized into long-range, short-range, and middle-range types based on their operational distance. Long-range locks allow access from several meters away, making them suitable for large areas. Short-range locks operate effectively within a few centimeters, providing enhanced security for high-value items. Middle-range locks bridge the gap, typically functioning within one to three meters, offering a balance of convenience and security. Each type caters to different access control needs and environments.</p></p>
