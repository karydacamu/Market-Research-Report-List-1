<p><strong>The Coolant Switch Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Way</li><li>3 Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coolant switch valve market is primarily categorized into two types: 2-way and 3-way valves. 2-way valves control the flow of coolant in a single direction, allowing for simple on/off operations, making them ideal for basic applications. In contrast, 3-way valves manage coolant flow from multiple sources, offering versatility by enabling mixing or diverting functionality. This flexibility is crucial in complex systems where precise temperature regulation and fluid control are required, leading to distinct applications across automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
