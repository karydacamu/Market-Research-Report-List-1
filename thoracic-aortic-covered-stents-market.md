<p><ul><li>Bifurcated Type</li><li>Straight Tube type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thoracic aortic covered stents market comprises primarily two types: bifurcated and straight tube stents. Bifurcated stents are designed to accommodate branches in the aorta, providing support to vessel bifurcations while ensuring blood flow to essential arteries. In contrast, straight tube stents are used for treating isolated thoracic aortic lesions, allowing for easier deployment and uniform expansion within the aorta. Both types play crucial roles in managing aortic diseases, enhancing patient outcomes through improved structural support and flow.</p></p>
