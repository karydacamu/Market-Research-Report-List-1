<p><p>The immunostimulatory adjuvants market comprises several key types. Aluminum hydroxide adjuvants, commonly known as alum, enhance immune responses by providing a depot effect and stimulating the production of antibodies. Liposomes, lipid-based carriers, improve the delivery and uptake of antigens, leading to stronger immune activation. Other adjuvants include microbial components and cytokines that further support immune responses. The choice of adjuvant significantly impacts vaccine efficacy and is critical for developing effective immunotherapies.</p></p>
