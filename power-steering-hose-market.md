<p><p>The power steering hose market is primarily divided into high-pressure and low-pressure categories. High-pressure power steering hoses are designed to withstand the intense pressure generated by the hydraulic system, ensuring effective steering response and control. In contrast, low-pressure power steering hoses operate with less pressure and are responsible for returning fluid to the reservoir. Both types are essential for the smooth functioning of power steering systems, contributing to improved vehicle maneuverability and driver comfort.</p></p>
