<p><p>The IoT LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) sensors market comprises two primary types: DC LVDT sensors and AC LVDT sensors. DC LVDT sensors utilize direct current for operation, enabling precise measurements with less susceptibility to noise, making them ideal for battery-powered devices. Conversely, AC LVDT sensors employ alternating current, offering high sensitivity and reliability in dynamic environments, ideal for industrial applications. Both types enhance measurement accuracy and are integral to smart IoT systems across various sectors, including manufacturing and automation.</p></p>
