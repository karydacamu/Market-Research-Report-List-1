<p><strong>The Metallic Effect Spray Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic effect spray paint market is divided into two primary types: solvent-based and water-based paints. Solvent-based metallic sprays offer high durability and a glossy finish, making them suitable for outdoor applications and industrial uses. They tend to dry quickly but can have strong fumes and require proper ventilation. Water-based metallic sprays, on the other hand, are eco-friendlier with low odor and easy cleanup. They are ideal for indoor projects and often provide a softer sheen, though they may not be as durable as their solvent-based counterparts.</p></p>
