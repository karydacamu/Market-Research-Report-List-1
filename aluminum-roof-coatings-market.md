<p><p>The aluminum roof coatings market is divided into two main types: fiber and non-fiber coatings. Fiber coatings incorporate reinforcing materials like fiberglass to enhance durability and tensile strength, offering increased resistance to wear and weather conditions. Non-fiber coatings, on the other hand, rely solely on aluminum-based compounds to provide reflective properties and UV protection, making them lightweight and easy to apply. Both types serve to extend the lifespan of roofing systems while improving energy efficiency through heat reflection.</p></p>
