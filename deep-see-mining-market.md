<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Deep See Mining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cobalt Rich Crusts (CRC)</li><li>Manganese Nodules</li><li>Seafloor Massive Sulphides (SMS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deep sea mining encompasses three primary market types: Cobalt Rich Crusts (CRC), Manganese Nodules, and Seafloor Massive Sulphides (SMS). CRCs are found on underwater mountains and are rich in cobalt and other metals, essential for batteries. Manganese Nodules, potato-shaped formations on the ocean floor, contain valuable metals like nickel, cobalt, and rare earth elements. SMS deposits, typically near hydrothermal vents, are concentrated in copper, zinc, and precious metals, making them significant for high-value resource extraction.</p></p>
