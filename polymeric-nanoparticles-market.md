<p><p>Polymeric nanoparticles are innovative delivery systems in the pharmaceutical and biomedical fields, mainly categorized into Dendrimers and Polyacrylamide (PAMAM) nanoparticles. DAB (Dendritic-Polymer) nanoparticles, characterized by their branched structures, provide high surface area and functionalization, enhancing drug encapsulation and release. PAMAM nanoparticles, known for their uniformity and versatile surface chemistry, facilitate targeted delivery and controlled release of therapeutic agents. Both types are crucial for improving drug efficacy and reducing side effects in various medical applications.</p></p>
