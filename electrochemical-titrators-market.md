<p><p>The electrochemical titrators market includes various types such as potentiometric titrators, volumetric titrators, and coulometric titrators. Potentiometric titrators measure the voltage change during a reaction, providing precise pH readings. Volumetric titrators dispense a titrant solution from a burette to achieve a specific endpoint, emphasizing accuracy in concentration. Coulometric titrators utilize the precise measurement of electric charge to determine the concentration of analytes. Other categories may include hybrid systems and specialized titrators designed for niche applications.</p></p>
