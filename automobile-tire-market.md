<p><strong>The Automobile Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile tire market is primarily divided into two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers of fabric plies that run diagonally, providing a flexible structure, making them suitable for certain applications like agriculture or off-road use, but they are less efficient on highways. In contrast, radial tires have plies arranged perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, improved traction, and enhanced durability, making them the preferred choice for most passenger vehicles in modern markets.</p></p>
