<p><ul><li>Electrostatic Chuck</li><li>Vacuum Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor chuck market encompasses various types, primarily electrostatic chucks and vacuum chucks. Electrostatic chucks utilize electrical forces to hold delicate silicon wafers securely during manufacturing processes, ensuring precise alignment and minimizing damage. In contrast, vacuum chucks employ suction to securely grip substrates, offering reliable stability for machining and lithography applications. Both types are critical in semiconductor fabrication, providing essential support for advanced manufacturing techniques and contributing to enhanced precision and efficiency in the production of microelectronic components.</p></p>
