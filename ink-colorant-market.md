<p><ul><li>Dye</li><li>Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ink colorant market primarily consists of two types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble colorants that impart vivid colors and are commonly used in water-based inks, offering excellent flow and transparency. However, they may have lower lightfastness. Pigments, on the other hand, are insoluble particles that provide better lightfastness and durability, making them suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Both types cater to distinct needs in various printing processes, influencing color quality and longevity.</p></p>
