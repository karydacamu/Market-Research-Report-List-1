<p><p>The surgical scrub brushes market includes two primary types: povidone-iodine scrub brushes and povidone-iodine-free scrub brushes. Povidone-iodine scrub brushes incorporate the antiseptic povidone-iodine, which effectively reduces microbial load on the skin before surgical procedures, enhancing infection control. In contrast, povidone-iodine-free scrub brushes use alternate antiseptics or antimicrobial agents, catering to individuals with sensitivities or allergies to iodine. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining sterile environments in healthcare settings, ensuring patient safety during surgeries.</p></p>
