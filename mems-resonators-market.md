<p><strong>The MEMS Resonators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Resonance Type</li><li>Parallel Resonance Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS resonators are categorized into series resonance and parallel resonance types based on their operational characteristics. Series resonance type MEMS resonators are designed to operate at the series resonant frequency, typically offering higher quality factors and better frequency stability, making them suitable for precision applications. In contrast, parallel resonance type MEMS resonators operate at the parallel resonant frequency, providing lower impedance and versatile integration options, which are ideal for oscillators and filters in consumer electronics. Both types serve distinct applications across various industries.</p></p>
