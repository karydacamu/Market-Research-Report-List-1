<p><p>The blood glucose testing market includes various technologies for measuring glucose levels. Glucose oxidase is the most commonly used method, relying on the enzymatic reaction of glucose with oxygen to produce hydrogen peroxide, which is then measured. Glucose dehydrogenase uses a different enzymatic process, often incorporating co-factors like NAD+ for increased accuracy. Other methods encompass alternative technologies such as electrochemical sensors and continuous glucose monitoring systems. Each type caters to different user needs, accuracy preferences, and testing environments.</p></p>
