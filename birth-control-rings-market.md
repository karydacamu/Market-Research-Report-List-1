<p><p>The birth control ring market includes three primary products: Estring, Femring, and NuvaRing. Estring is primarily used for hormone therapy in postmenopausal women, releasing estrogen to alleviate symptoms like vaginal dryness. Femring also provides estrogen but is designed for treating menopausal symptoms. In contrast, NuvaRing serves as a contraceptive device, releasing a combination of hormones (estrogen and progestin) to prevent ovulation. These rings vary in application and target audience, highlighting diverse approaches to hormonal management and contraception.</p></p>
