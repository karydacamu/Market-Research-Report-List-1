Impact Nutsetters utilize a hammering action to drive nuts and bolts quickly, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications where speed and torque are essential. In contrast, Torsion Nutsetters focus on delivering controlled torque, providing precision in fastening while minimizing the risk of over-tightening. Both types serve distinct needs in various industries, enhancing efficiency and performance in assembly tasks.</p></p>
