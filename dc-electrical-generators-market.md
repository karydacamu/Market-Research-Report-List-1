<p><strong>The DC Electrical Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Generators</li><li>Series Generators</li><li>Compound Wound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC electrical generators are categorized into three main types based on their winding configurations: shunt generators, series generators, and compound-wound generators. Shunt generators connect the field winding in parallel with the armature, offering stable voltage under varying loads. Series generators have their field winding in series with the armature, providing high current for applications requiring significant power output. Compound-wound generators combine both series and shunt windings, allowing for versatile performance, making them suitable for various load conditions and applications.</p></p>
