<p><strong>The Atomised Ferro Silico Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Atomised</li><li>Gas Atomised</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Atomised Ferro Silico Powder market is divided into two main types: Water Atomised and Gas Atomised. Water Atomised powder is produced by rapidly cooling molten metal with water, resulting in finer particles with higher purity and controlled size, making it suitable for applications requiring precision. Gas Atomised powder, on the other hand, utilizes inert gas to disperse molten metal, producing spherical particles with better flow characteristics. Both types cater to diverse industries, including metallurgy and additive manufacturing, based on their distinct properties.</p></p>
