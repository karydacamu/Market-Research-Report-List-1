<p><p>The medical transfer boards market comprises two main types: with rollers and without rollers. Transfer boards with rollers facilitate smoother transfers by reducing friction, making it easier for caregivers to move patients between surfaces, enhancing comfort and safety. Conversely, transfer boards without rollers rely on a flat, sturdy surface for manual sliding, offering a simple and cost-effective solution. Both types are essential in healthcare settings, aiding in patient mobility and ensuring safer transfers for individuals with limited mobility.</p></p>
