<p><ul><li>Threaded End</li><li>Seal Welded End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Welded bonnet gate valves are categorized into two main market types: threaded end and seal welded end. Threaded end valves feature connections that are joined via threaded fittings, allowing for easy installation and disassembly. In contrast, seal welded end valves utilize a permanent welding method to create a leak-proof seal, enhancing durability and pressure tolerance. Both types are widely used in various industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing, due to their reliable performance and strength.</p></p>
