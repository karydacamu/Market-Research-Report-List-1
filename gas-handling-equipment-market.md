<p><p>The gas handling equipment market comprises four main types: generation, storage, detection, and transportation. Generation focuses on producing gases, typically for energy or industrial use. Storage involves safely holding gases in containers or tanks to prevent leakage and ensure availability. Detection encompasses instruments that monitor gas levels and identify leaks, crucial for safety and compliance. Transportation refers to the methods and equipment used to move gases from one location to another, ensuring efficiency and maintaining integrity throughout the process.</p></p>
